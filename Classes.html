Bodybuilding



bodybuilding, a regimen of exercises designed to enhance the human body's muscular
development and promote general health and fitness. As a competitive activity,
bodybuilding aims to display in artistic fashion pronounced muscle mass, symmetry,
and definition for overall aesthetic effect.

By engaging in physical activities such as weight training and aerobic exercise
you have more chance to reduce and control high blood pressure, obesity and high cholesterol.
Bodybuilding has such great and healthy impact on muscle, bones and joints.
Bodybuilding keeps your body and muscle strong and flexible.
